b r o w n i e s

description

these brownies are fudgy and have a soft and smooth texture with a deep buttery and chocolaty flavor

ingredients

butter mixture:

  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter
  • 1 tablespoon espresso powder
  • 1 coffee ice cube

batter mixture:

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla paste or extract
  • 2-1/4 cups granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup Dutch-processed cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoon espresso powder
  • 1 tablespoon kosher salt
  • 8 ounces 75% cacao chocolate, melted
  • 1-1/2 cups all-purpose flour

chopped chocolate:

  • 3-1/2 ounces chopped milk chocolate
  • 3-1/2 ounces chopped 75% cacao chocolate
  • 6 ounces chopped 75% cacao chocolate, for the topping

brownie batter

  1. brown the butter to give it a deeper color and flavor
  2. stir espresso powder into the browned butter (this blooms the flavor)
  3. add coffee ice cube to cool the mixture
  4. while the butter cools, eggs and sugar are whipped until light before adding the cocoa powder, more espresso powder, salt, flour and melted chocolate
  5. the espresso-butter mixture is then stirred in to create a ridiculously rich batter
  6. stir in the chopped dark and milk chocolate pieces, then spoon the batter into a pan (13 x 9-in. for thin brownies, or an 8-in. square pan for thicker slabs)
  7. sprinkle more chopped, dark chocolate over the top

wait 1

  1. wrap that pan and put it in the fridge for 72 hours...
    • this allows the flavors more time to set
    • it lets flour fully hydrate, giving the batter a deeper color and creating brownies that are dense and moist
    • also, having cold batter will keep it from over-baking

bake, then wait 2

  1. after 72 hours in the refrigerator, they go straight into a 350Â°F oven to bake for 30 minutes (or 45 minutes if you're making thicker brownies in a square pan.)
    • note: when you pull the pan from the oven, the brownies will be wobbly in the center
  2. cover the top of the pan in foil and place in the freezer for 30 minutes. this will help keep moisture in the brownies
  3. after 30 minutes, the brownies go back into the fridge for 24 hours
